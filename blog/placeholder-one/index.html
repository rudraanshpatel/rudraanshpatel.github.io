One-sentence hook that sets up the rest. Italic, slightly larger.

Open with the concrete situation or observation. Keep paragraphs short and rhythmic. White space is your friend.

Build the argument one paragraph at a time. Each paragraph should make one move — introduce a fact, draw a contrast, ask a question.

Use h2 for section breaks

Use h2 sparingly — not every paragraph needs a header. The plain <hr /> below is another option for a visual break without a label.

End the post when you've said the thing. Don't keep going to "wrap up." Readers know.

Use a blockquote to land the one line you want people to remember.
