What that actually means

No pace pressure. No mandatory distance. No serious-runner vibes. We're a community first, a run club second. Show up in any shoes you own — we'll do the rest. Stay for the coffee, the Zumba, the pool, whatever this week's after-party is.

If you want a training plan, there are other clubs. If you want a Sunday morning that makes Monday easier — you're in the right place.
