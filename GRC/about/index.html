Our story

Six friends.
One Sunday.
February 2024.

We're a community for the generation that runs together — and parties together right after. No coach. No training plan. No fee. Just a Sunday morning we've never missed.

We started this because running alone
was boring.
78 weeks later we still show up.
— The founders
Why we exist

Running is better
with a party.

Most run clubs are about pace, splits, and PRs. That's great if you're chasing one. Most people aren't.

GRC is for everyone else — the people who want to move on Sunday morning, but also dance, swim, eat, and laugh with strangers who become friends by week three.

Our 3K is the same as your 3K. Our DJ set is the same as everyone's DJ set. The thing that changes is who's standing next to you when it ends.
