The home chapter

78 weeks. Zero skips.

Bhopal started with six friends meeting at Boat Club every Sunday. Three months in we had 30 people. By the end of year one, 120. We've never missed a Sunday — not in monsoon, not in 47°C, not on Holi, not when half the city had food poisoning after a wedding.

Now we rotate between Bhojpur Club, Taj Lakefront, Upper Lake, and pool venues across the city. Some Sundays it's a 3K easy. Some Sundays it's a full rave. You'll know by Saturday night.

The sister city

When one city isn't enough.

Two of our Bhopal regulars moved to Indore in mid-2025 and refused to give up the Sunday habit. So they started a chapter. Vishal Singh leads the Indore Bollywood Zumba — he's a force.

Indore Sundays lean harder into the recovery side: 3K easy run, pool sessions, ice plunges, pilates. Less DJ, more dance floor.
